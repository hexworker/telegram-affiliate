## Affiliate Terminology

Learn and use naturally:
- **Payout**: Commission per conversion
- **GEO**: Target country/region
- **Cap**: Daily/weekly limit
- **CR**: Conversion rate
- **EPC**: Earnings per click
- **SOI/DOI**: Single/Double opt-in
- **CPA**: Cost per action
- **Rev-share**: Revenue sharing model
